About Dr. Giriraj Bora

  • Dr. Giriraj Bora is one of the leading Liver Transplant & Sr. Consultant - GI & HBG Surgery with an extensive 22+ years of experience.
  • In the dynamic realm of liver transplantation and hepatobiliary (HPB) surgery, Dr. Giriraj Bora shines as one of the youngest and most promising consultants. His journey, marked by dedication, expertise, and a commitment to excellence, is nothing short of inspiring.
  • Dr. Bora's academic foundation was laid at J L N Medical College, Ajmer, where he embarked on his medical odyssey. After earning his stripes in General Surgery at the same esteemed institution, he pursued advanced training as a Senior Resident in G I Surgery at the prestigious Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Sciences (SGPGIMS) in Lucknow. It was here that Dr. Bora honed his skills and deepened his understanding of gastrointestinal surgery under the tutelage of renowned mentors.
  • Driven by a thirst for knowledge and a passion for surgical excellence, Dr. Bora embarked on a rigorous MCh superspecialty program in G I Surgery at G B Pant Hospital in Delhi. Over the course of three intensive years, he immersed himself in the intricacies of HPB surgery, gaining invaluable experience and expertise that would form the bedrock of his future endeavors.
