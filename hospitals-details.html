Artemis Hospital Gurugram

Book appointment
Gurugram 350 Beds Established in : 2007 Super Specialty

Artemis Hospital, inaugurated in 2007 and nestled in Gurugram, India, spans over 9 acres of modern infrastructure. With over 350 beds, it stands as a pinnacle of healthcare excellence, offering state-of-the-art facilities and services across various specialties. Notably, Artemis Hospital holds the prestigious distinction of being the first in Gurugram to earn accreditation from both JCI (Joint Commission International) and NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers), reflecting its commitment to quality care and patient safety.

Artemis stands as a beacon of advanced healthcare in India, meticulously designed to offer a wide array of cutting-edge medical and surgical interventions. Boasting a comprehensive range of inpatient and outpatient services, it blends modern technology with the expertise of distinguished doctors hailing from India and abroad. The hospital's medical practices are deeply rooted in research and consistently benchmarked against global standards, ensuring the delivery of world-class care. Coupled with a warm, patient-centric environment, Artemis Hospital is renowned for its top-notch services and affordability, earning it a well-deserved reputation as one of the most respected healthcare institutions in the country.

team and specialities

  • Artemis Hospital boasts a distinguished team of 300 full-time doctors, each specializing in various fields, ensuring comprehensive medical expertise across the spectrum of healthcare. With 11 centers of excellence and a remarkable array of 40 specialties, Artemis is equipped to address diverse healthcare needs with precision and excellence.
  • Artemis Hospital prides itself on providing access to cutting-edge equipment for predictive, diagnostic, and therapeutic imaging. With a commitment to staying at the forefront of medical technology, Artemis offers some of the most advanced imaging equipment available. This includes state-of-the-art tools for predictive analysis, precise diagnostics, and effective therapeutic interventions, ensuring patients receive the highest standard of care possible.
  • Artemis Hospital is equipped with the latest in medical technology, including advanced equipment such as the 3 Tesla MRI, 64 Slice Cardiac CT Scan, and 16-slice PET CT from Philips Medical System. These cutting-edge imaging systems enable precise and detailed diagnostic imaging, facilitating accurate diagnoses and tailored treatment plans for patients. With such state-of-the-art equipment, Artemis Hospital remains committed to delivering the highest standards of healthcare with precision and efficiency.
